About Us

Dedicated to Community Wellness

Empowering health through trusted clinical care and family support

At Bliss Bangladesh, we operate as a leading authorized EPI (Expanded Program on Immunization) and Family Planning center. Our mission is simple: to make critical healthcare services accessible, comfortable, and reliable for families across Bangladesh.

With modern medical equipment, highly trained healthcare professionals, and a warm, inviting environment, we ensure that both children and adults receive world-class preventative and diagnostic care.
